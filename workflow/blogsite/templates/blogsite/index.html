Health is a fundamental aspect of human well-being, encompassing physical, mental, and social dimensions. It is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. Physical health involves maintaining a sound body through regular exercise, proper nutrition, adequate sleep, and avoiding harmful habits. Mental health refers to our emotional and psychological well-being, encompassing factors such as stress management, self-care, and seeking support when needed.
